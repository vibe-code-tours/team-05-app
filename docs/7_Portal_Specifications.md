# Portal Specifications

## 1. Admin Panel
The Admin Panel is the central control hub for the entire platform.

### 1.1 User Management
- View list of all registered Clients and Sellers.
- Approve or Reject new Seller applications.
- Ban or suspend users violating platform policies.

### 1.2 Catalog Management
- Create, Edit, and Delete Categories and Sub-categories.
- Manage global Brands.
- Review and manually approve/reject pending product listings from Sellers (if they do not meet auto-approval criteria).

### 1.3 Order & Dispute Management
- View all platform orders.
- Mediate disputes between Clients and Sellers (e.g., missing cargo, damaged goods).
- Manually override order statuses if necessary.

### 1.4 Reports & Analytics
- Dashboard showing daily active users, total GMV (Gross Merchandise Value), and commission earned.
- View top-performing Sellers and popular Categories.

## 2. Seller Portal
A dedicated dashboard for verified Sales Persons to manage their business.

### 2.1 Dashboard
- Overview of Pending Orders, Items to Pack, and Items in Transit.
- Revenue summary (Pending vs. Cleared earnings).

### 2.2 Product Management
- Interface to upload new products (In Stock, Cargo).
- Special interface for creating "Promotion Opportunities" with a time limit.
- Manage variants, pricing, and stock levels.

### 2.3 Order Fulfillment
- View incoming orders and uploaded payment slips.
- Update order statuses (e.g., mark as 'Purchased', 'Packed', 'Sent to Cargo').
- Export packing lists for the day.

## 3. Customer Portal
The end-user interface for browsing and managing personal activity.

### 3.1 Profile & Settings
- Manage personal details, phone number, and delivery addresses.
- View saved payment preferences.

### 3.2 Order Tracking
- Dedicated "Active Orders" page displaying the Cargo Timeline (e.g., visual progress bar from Bangkok to Yangon).
- View order history and past invoices.

### 3.3 Wishlist & Reviews
- Save products for later (Wishlist).
- Interface to submit ratings and reviews for completed orders.
