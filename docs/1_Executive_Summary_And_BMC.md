# 1. Executive Summary

**Project Name:** CrossMart
**Vision:** To become Myanmar's most trusted Cross-Border Marketplace Platform.
**Mission:** To unify Verified Seller Ecosystems, Transparent Cargo Tracking, Authentic Products, Competitive Pricing, and Local + Cross-border Commerce under a single, secure platform.

**Overview:**
CrossMart aims to solve the current fragmentation in the Myanmar e-commerce market, where Facebook Pages, Viber groups, and Messenger are primarily used for pre-orders and cargo services. By consolidating these into a single platform, CrossMart provides a secure and transparent environment for buyers (Clients) and verified sellers (Sales Persons). It handles distinct product types including In Stock, Cargo items, and Promotion items, ensuring transparent tracking from order placement to final delivery.

# 2. Business Model Canvas (BMC)

## 1. Customer Segments
- **Buyers (Clients):** Individuals in Myanmar looking for authentic, competitively priced products (local or international) with reliable tracking.
- **Sellers (Sales Persons):** Verified individuals or businesses who have access to stock, can facilitate cross-border cargo (e.g., from Bangkok), or have opportunities to buy items on promotion.

## 2. Value Proposition
- **For Buyers:** Easy discovery, trust (verified sellers), transparent cargo tracking, clear order statuses, and price competitiveness.
- **For Sellers:** Organized order management, automated approval workflows, promotion listing tools, and an established trust platform to grow their business.

## 3. Channels
- Web Application (Phase 1)
- Android App (Phase 2)
- Social Media Marketing (Facebook, Viber integrations for sharing)

## 4. Customer Relationships
- Automated tracking updates & notifications
- Reviews and Ratings for Seller credibility
- Trusted intermediation (Admin moderation)

## 5. Revenue Streams
- **Commission Fees:** Percentage of successful transactions.
- **Featured Listings:** Premium placement for Sellers' products or promotions.
- **Logistics Partnerships:** Potential revenue share with cargo providers.

## 6. Key Resources
- **Technology Platform:** Next.js, NestJS backend, Database (PostgreSQL).
- **Verified Sellers Network:** The initial seed of trusted sellers.
- **Admin & Support Team:** Moderating sellers and handling disputes.

## 7. Key Activities
- Platform development and maintenance.
- Seller Verification and Approval.
- Cargo Tracking Integration.
- Order and Payment processing management.

## 8. Key Partnerships
- **Cargo / Logistics Providers:** (e.g., Air cargo from Bangkok to Myanmar).
- **Payment Gateways:** Local Myanmar payment methods (KPay, WavePay).

## 9. Cost Structure
- **Development & Hosting:** Cloudflare R2, Server hosting. (AWS deferred to Phase 3/4).
- **Operations:** Admin and Customer Support salaries.
- **Marketing:** User acquisition campaigns.
